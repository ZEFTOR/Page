STEAM

Steam was created by Valve Corporationand presents itself as ‘the ultimate online gaming platform’. It is certainly one of the most complete packages on offer. You will find over 70,000 titles and a community of tens of millions of players. There are other features such as the mod workshop, live broadcasts, guides, forums, game exchanges, and a Big Picture mode (where you can interact with the interface with a games controller). Also in October of this year, they created a mobile version of the store and broke the record for the number of concurrent users online(30,032,005 users).
